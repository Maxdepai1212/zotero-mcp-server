## 🚀 Getting Started
Welcome to the zotero-mcp-server! This application helps you manage your research references. It uses the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to work seamlessly with Zotero, making it easy to search, cite, and organize your research.

## 🌐 Using zotero-mcp-server
Once you open the application, you will see the main dashboard. Here you can:

- **Search for References:** Enter keywords or titles to find the research papers you need.
- **Cite Appropriately:** Use the built-in tools to create citations in various formats such as APA, MLA, Chicago, and more.
- **Organize Your Library:** Create folders, and tag documents for easy access.
- **Sync with Zotero:** Connect your Zotero account to automatically sync your references.

## 💡 Features
- **Intuitive Interface:** Easy navigation for all users, regardless of skill level.
- **Real-time Collaboration:** Share your research with teammates instantly.
- **Customizable Tags:** Create a tagging system that works for you.
- **Smart Search Functionality:** Get fast and accurate results as you type.
